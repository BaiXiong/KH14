Qhov Tseeb

“All truths are easy to understand once they are discovered; the point is to discover them.” That is the Path of Knowledge and Understanding. KEEB HMOOB yog ib yam ntawm spiritual oneness. What is spiritual oneness? Spiritual oneness means the incarnated soul (Plig) is achieving a higher degree of oneness with the higher soul (Ntsuj Keeb), and a certain degree of oneness with GOD (YAWM SAUB) and oneness with all.

There are many paths to achieving illumination and spiritual oneness. One of the paths is Understanding. This is called TAUB IB ZEEJ NTAWM ZEEJ NTSUJ. This path is the easies path for those who are mentally developed. Yog li ntawd nyob rau hauv VAJ LOOG KEEB HMOOB peb thiaj li muaj ob peb txheej txuj los cob qhia. Txuj KEEB HMOOB of Knowledge and Understanding also requires physical purification and inner purification character building. Without physical purification, the body may bcome sick. Without inner purification the individual may become psychologically imbalanced.

It is also important to generate good karma by doing good and service in order to offset one's ancient negative karma. Txhua yam tseeb yog pib los ntawm los lus “KUV” no los ntag. Los lus “KUV” no muaj peev xwm pab tau rau yus ua neej zoo txhua yam huv tib si. Tab sis “Kuv” yog ib lo lus uas nrhuav tshem txhua yam huv tib si thiab. Yog li ua ntej peb mus kawm deb tshaj nov ntxiv, cia peb los kawm txog txoj kev tsa peb tus xob zeej ua ntej tso. No yog ib txoj ntawm ob peb txoj kev ua tsa yus tus xob zeej kom sawv. Yog tias peb ua tib zoo xyaum raws li ram nov xwb peb yeej muaj peev xwm hloov tau peb tus xob zeej tsis ciaj sia mus kom ciaj sia tau. 10 nqe lus tsa koj tus xob zeej muaj xws li:

  1. Xav qhov uas zoo
  2. Yas suab
  3. Qhov chaw koj nyob
  4. Khoom tseem ceeb ceev cia
  5. Yam koj cia siab, kev ntseeg
  6. Txeej teg pwv xyaum
  7. Ceev yam yeeb yuj koj nyiam
  8. Saib yam uas yuav muaj yeej
  9. Ua luam dej, da dej
  10. Xyaum ua zoo, hlub
  11. Paub koj tus kheej
  12. Luag zoo siab
